基础数学公式

指数和对数

给定实数a>0, m, n，有以下恒等式：

\begin{aligned} \left(a^{m}\right)^{n} &=a^{m n} \\ a^{m} a^{n} &=a^{m+n} \end{aligned}

对数

\begin{aligned} a &=b^{\log _{b} a} \\ \log _{c}(a b) &=\log _{c} a+\log _{c} b \\ \log _{b} a^{n} &=n \log _{b} a \\ \log _{b} a &=\frac{\log _{c} a}{\log _{c} b} \\ \log _{b} a &=\frac{1}{\log _{a} b} \\ a^{\log _{b} c} &=c^{\log _{b} a} \end{aligned}

组合数学的常用公式

\begin{aligned} C_{n}^{m}&=C_{n-1}^{m-1}+C_{n-1}^{m} \\ m C_{n}^{m}&=n C_{n-1}^{m-1} \\ C_{n}^{0}+C_{n}^{1}+C_{n}^{2}+\ldots \ldots+C_{n}^{n}&=2^{n} \\ 1 C_{n}^{1}+2 C_{n}^{2}+3 C_{n}^{3}+\ldots \ldots+n C_{n}^{n}&=n 2^{n-1} \\ 1^{2} C_{n}^{1}+2^{2} C_{n}^{2}+3^{2} C_{n}^{3}+\ldots . .+n^{2} C_{n}^{n}&=n(n+1) 2^{n-2} \\ \frac{C_{n}^{1}}{1}-\frac{C_{n}^{2}}{2}+\frac{C_{n}^{3}}{3}+\ldots \ldots+(-1)^{n-1} \frac{C_{n}^{n}}{n}&=1+\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{3}+\ldots \ldots+\frac{1}{n} \\ \left(C_{n}^{0}\right)^{2}+\left(C_{n}^{1}\right)^{2}+\left(C_{n}^{2}\right)^{2}+\ldots \ldots+\left(C_{n}^{n}\right)^{2}&=C_{2 n}^{n} \end{aligned}
