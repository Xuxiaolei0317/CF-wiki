组合数学的常用公式

C_{n}^{m}=C_{n-1}^{m-1}+C_{n-1}^{m} m C_{n}^{m}=n C_{n-1}^{m-1} C_{n}^{0}+C_{n}^{1}+C_{n}^{2}+\ldots \ldots+C_{n}^{n}=2^{n} 1 C_{n}^{1}+2 C_{n}^{2}+3 C_{n}^{3}+\ldots \ldots+n C_{n}^{n}=n 2^{n-1} 1^{2} C_{n}^{1}+2^{2} C_{n}^{2}+3^{2} C_{n}^{3}+\ldots . .+n^{2} C_{n}^{n}=n(n+1) 2^{n-2} \frac{C_{n}^{1}}{1}-\frac{C_{n}^{2}}{2}+\frac{C_{n}^{3}}{3}+\ldots \ldots+(-1)^{n-1} \frac{C_{n}^{n}}{n}=1+\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{3}+\ldots \ldots+\frac{1}{n} \left(C_{n}^{0}\right)^{2}+\left(C_{n}^{1}\right)^{2}+\left(C_{n}^{2}\right)^{2}+\ldots \ldots+\left(C_{n}^{n}\right)^{2}=C_{2 n}^{n}
